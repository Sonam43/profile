Welcome to Sherubtse College

Sherubtse College, Bhutan’s oldest institute of higher learning, is located in the serene and beautiful environment of Kanglung. We provide an exceptional academic experience through a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs tailored to the needs of students.

Our dedicated faculty, cutting-edge facilities, and vibrant campus life create an ideal environment for holistic learning. Join us to embark on a journey of personal and intellectual growth.
